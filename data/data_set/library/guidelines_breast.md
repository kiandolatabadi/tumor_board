# Breast Oncology Guideline Reference (Internal, Fictional)
*Synthetic guideline library for hackathon demo purposes. Grading follows the Class of Recommendation (I-IV) x Level of Evidence (A/B/C) convention. Entirely fabricated — not derived from, and not a substitute for, any real published guideline.*

## G-BR-01 — Fertility preservation counseling
Reproductive-age patients (generally under 45) starting therapy with gonadotoxic potential (anthracycline/alkylator-based chemotherapy, ovarian suppression) should be offered referral to reproductive endocrinology for fertility preservation counseling **before** treatment initiation, regardless of stated childbearing plans at the time.
**Class I, Level B.** Applies equally to first-line and re-treatment/escalation settings — a deferral documented at an earlier treatment line does not exempt a later line from re-offering the conversation.

## G-BR-02 — Endocrine-based therapy vs. cytotoxic re-challenge at first distant recurrence
For ER-positive/HER2-negative metastatic recurrence with non-visceral or lower-volume visceral disease and no imminent organ threat, endocrine therapy plus a CDK4/6 inhibitor is preferred over cytotoxic chemotherapy re-challenge as first escalation.
**Class IIa, Level A.** The choice between the two pathways should carry a documented clinical rationale (tumor burden, prior therapy exposure, patient preference, comorbidity) — an undocumented choice is a process gap independent of whether the choice itself was reasonable.

## G-BR-03 — Re-biopsy at first distant recurrence
Histologic and receptor (ER/PR/HER2) reconfirmation at the site of first distant recurrence is recommended, since receptor status can convert from the primary tumor.
**Class I, Level B.**

## G-BR-04 — Cardiac risk with anthracycline re-exposure
Cumulative anthracycline dose and baseline cardiac function (LVEF, prior cardiac or autoimmune-cardiac history) must be reassessed before any anthracycline-containing regimen is reconsidered in a patient with prior anthracycline exposure or a pre-existing cardiac condition.
**Class I, Level B.**

## G-BR-05 — Goals-of-care review at change in disease status
A goals-of-care conversation should be revisited whenever disease status changes materially (e.g., transition from curative-intent to recurrent/metastatic disease), even when a goals-of-care discussion is already on file from an earlier phase of treatment.
**Class IIa, Level C.**

## G-BR-06 — Germline testing relevance to systemic therapy selection
Germline BRCA1/2 status should be confirmed (resulted, not pending) before offering a PARP-inhibitor-based regimen or trial; a pending result must be explicitly disclosed as a limiting factor in any recommendation contingent on it.
**Class I, Level B.**
