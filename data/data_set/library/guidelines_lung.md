# Lung Oncology Guideline Reference (Internal, Fictional, thin — control case only)

## G-LU-01 — First-line therapy selection by biomarker status
For stage IV NSCLC without a targetable driver mutation (EGFR/ALK/ROS1 negative) and PD-L1 tumor proportion score of 50% or higher, single-agent anti-PD-1 therapy is an appropriate first-line option. **Class I, Level A.**

## G-LU-02 — Goals-of-care conversation at initial stage IV diagnosis
A goals-of-care conversation should occur at or shortly after initial diagnosis of metastatic disease, prior to treatment initiation. **Class I, Level B.**
