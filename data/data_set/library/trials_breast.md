## Trial BR-2201 — Ribociclib + Fulvestrant Post-Progression Registry Study
- **Population:** ER-positive/HER2-negative metastatic breast cancer, first distant recurrence, no prior CDK4/6 inhibitor exposure
- **Key eligibility:** ECOG 0-1; measurable or evaluable disease; adequate hepatic/renal function; no more than 1 prior chemotherapy line for metastatic disease
- **Recruitment status:** **Closed to new enrollment as of 2026-04-01** (accrual target met)
- **Site:** Coordinating site — Meridian Regional Cancer Center, ~310 miles from patient's home site; no local satellite site open
- **Notes:** Still referenced informally by clinicians as "the ribociclib trial" from its earlier open-enrollment period — the name persisting in conversation outlasted its actual availability.

## Trial BR-1187 — PARP Inhibitor Maintenance in Germline BRCA-Mutant Metastatic Breast Cancer
- **Population:** Metastatic breast cancer, confirmed germline BRCA1/2 pathogenic variant, at most 2 prior lines of therapy
- **Key eligibility:** Confirmed germline BRCA1/2 mutation **(resulted, required — not presumptive)**; ECOG 0-1; no active second malignancy
- **Recruitment status:** Open, actively enrolling
- **Site:** Local site open (same health system)
- **Notes:** A "sent" or "pending" germline test does not meet the eligibility bar as written; only a resulted, positive test qualifies.

## Trial BR-0942 — Capecitabine Re-Challenge Combination Study
- **Population:** ER-positive/HER2-negative metastatic breast cancer, prior anthracycline/taxane exposure
- **Key eligibility:** Adequate cardiac and hepatic function; ECOG 0-2; current (within 30 days) renal function panel required for dosing
- **Recruitment status:** Open, actively enrolling
- **Site:** Local site open
